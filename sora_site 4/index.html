About SØRA

SØRA started as a project between close friends who wanted to celebrate their bonds through design. Each character you see on this site is inspired by a real person and their favourite quote or catchphrase. Our mission is to bring their stories to life through immersive experiences and playful fashion. We’re building a community around friendship, creativity and wearable storytelling—join us on this journey!

About the founder: This site was designed and built by the founding member of the SØRA crew as a labour of love. If you have questions, feedback or partnership ideas, feel free to reach out at joshuaistiroti@gmail.com.

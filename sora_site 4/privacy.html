Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

When you sign up to join our waitlist, we collect your email address solely for the purpose of notifying you about product launches and updates. We do not sell or share your email with third parties.

When you participate in our quizzes or trivia, we store your name and score in your browser’s local storage so that you can view the leaderboard. This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Use of Cookies & Local Storage

We may use cookies or local storage to remember your preferences and store quiz/trivia scores. These small data files do not contain personal information beyond what you explicitly provide.

Third‑Party Services

In the future, our store will be integrated with Printful to fulfil orders. When you place an order through Printful, you will be subject to Printful’s privacy policy and terms of service.
